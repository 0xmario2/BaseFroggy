Welcome to BaseFroggy

📖 Introduction

BaseFroggy is a strategy game built on the Base blockchain network. Here you can build and manage your own kingdom, collect and manage natural resources, train a powerful army of frogs, fight against opponents, and expand your territory to become the strongest ruler in the swamp world.

Start with a simple village, upgrade your buildings to increase production, prepare a strong army, and prove your skills to become the most respected leader. All your progress is saved securely and can be accessed anytime.
