🎮 About the Game

BaseFroggy is an exciting strategy video game built on the Base blockchain network. You step into the role of a leader in a vast swamp world, where you must build your village, gather resources, train your army of frogs, and fight against other players to expand your territory.

Every decision you make shapes your kingdom. Start small, upgrade your buildings, unlock new technologies, and become the most powerful ruler in the land. All progress is saved securely on the blockchain, meaning your achievements are yours forever.

✨ Main Features

  • 🏗️ Build & Upgrade: Construct mines, water pumps, barracks, and more to boost your production and strength.
  • 📦 Resource Management: Collect Gold, Water, and Food to grow your village and train troops.
  • ⚔️ Army System: Train Warrior Frogs and Elite Frogs, each with unique stats and abilities.
  • 💥 Battle Mode: Fight against enemies to earn rewards, experience, and increase your rank.
  • 📅 Daily Missions: Log in every day to claim free rewards and bonuses.
  • 🔗 Blockchain Powered: Connect your wallet, own your progress, and trade assets in the future.

📖 How to Play

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Use MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet to connect to the Base network. This keeps your progress safe and allows you to access the game anytime.

Step 2: Build Your Village

Start by upgrading your Gold Mine and Water Pump to generate resources automatically. More resources mean faster growth!

Step 3: Train Your Army

Use Food to train Warrior Frogs and Elite Frogs. A stronger army gives you a higher chance to win battles and earn big rewards.

Step 4: Fight & Grow

Go into battle, defeat opponents, and collect Gold and Food. Use these to expand your village and unlock new features.
